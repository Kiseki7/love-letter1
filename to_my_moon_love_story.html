To My Moon
Chapter I
The Beginning of an Unexpected Story
If someone had told me that one day I would fall hopelessly in love with a girl because of an IT ticket, I would have laughed and carried on with my day.
Yet that is exactly where our story began.
The first time I heard your name was when a ticket landed on my queue. It had been raised by a girl named Tabitha. As was often the case in my busy days, the ticket did not have enough information, so I closed it and directed it elsewhere.
I thought nothing of it.
But fate, as I have come to learn, has a peculiar sense of humour.
The issue eventually found its way back to me, and that was when I called you on Teams. We spoke, resolved the issue, and that should have been the end of it.
Yet somehow, it wasn't. There was something about your voice.
Even now, I cannot quite explain it.
I only know that when the call ended, I wished it had lasted a little longer. 💜
Chapter II
The Spark
A few days later, I happened to walk through the call centre when someone called your name.
I looked. Just for a moment.
Just long enough.
And something shifted inside me.
There were no fireworks. No dramatic music. No grand declaration from the heavens.
Just a quiet certainty.
A feeling so strange and so strong that I could not ignore it.
Something deep within me said:
"There she is."
From that moment on, I wanted to know you.
Not because of how you looked.
Not because of what anyone told me about you.
But because something about you felt different.
And I could not stop thinking about it.
Chapter III
Finding My Way to You
I secretly hoped another ticket would come my way so I would have a reason to talk to you again.
It never did.
So I found other ways.
A Teams message here. A conversation there.
An attempt to keep the connection alive.
Some worked better than others.
But eventually, I got your number.
And wanting to know you better, I asked a simple question:
"Do you prefer calls or texts?"
When you said calls, I smiled.
Because it meant I would get to hear your voice more often.
And so we talked.
About life.
About work.
About nothing and everything.
And somehow those conversations became the best part of my day. 🌙
Chapter IV
The Heartbreak Before the Beginning
Then came the news I never wanted to hear.
You told me you had a boyfriend.
I wish I could say I took it well.
The truth is, I was crushed.
Because by then, I was already falling for you.
I tried to convince myself to move on.
I tried to be sensible.
But every time we spoke, every time you laughed, every time I heard your voice, I found myself right back where I started.
Wanting more time with you.
Wanting more conversations.
Wanting you.
And despite everything, there was still a small voice inside me that refused to give up.
A voice that kept saying:
"Wait."
Chapter V
The Day Everything Changed
Then slowly, somehow, we found our way back to each other.
The conversations returned.
The connection grew stronger.
The chemistry was undeniable.
And every day I became more convinced that what I felt was real.
Until finally, on the 17th of May, everything changed.
The day we went on our first date.
What makes that memory even funnier is that I was absolutely broke at the time.
A terrible situation for a man planning a date.
But I did not care.
Because I had already waited long enough.
I was terrified that if I delayed, someone else would find what I had found in you.
And I could not bear that thought.
I did not want to ask you over a text.
I did not want to ask you over a phone call.
I wanted to look you in the eyes and ask properly.
Truthfully, I was not prepared for a "no."
That answer would have destroyed me.
But then you said yes.
And in that moment, every worry disappeared. 💜
Chapter VI
Home
Since my last love story ended, I had spent nearly two years convincing myself that perhaps will never be able to love again.
I met people.
Friends tried to introduce me to others.
I gave opportunities a chance.
Yet no matter how hard I tried, I never felt that connection.
Not once.
Then I met you.
And suddenly everything made sense.
You never felt forced.
You never felt temporary.
You never felt like someone I was trying to convince myself to love.
You simply felt right.
Most people enter relationships carefully, leaving room for disappointment.
Leaving room for an exit.
Leaving room for doubt.
But with you, I do not feel doubt.
I feel peace.
I feel certainty.
I feel home.
Chapter VII
Why I Call You Moon
Have you ever wondered why I call you Moon?
It is because before you, my heart had been living in darkness for a very long time.
Not the dramatic kind.
Just the quiet darkness that comes when you stop believing that something beautiful is waiting for you.
Then you appeared.
Like moonlight breaking through the night sky.
Soft.
Beautiful.
Steady.
You did not force your way into my life.
You simply illuminated it.
You reminded me that love could still be genuine.
That happiness could still be real.
That my heart was still capable of feeling deeply.
You became the light that guided me back to myself.
And for that, I will always be grateful. 💜
Happy Anniversary,
Princess
I choose you. Today. Tomorrow.
The day after, and every day that follows.

I choose your laughter. I choose your smile.
I choose your hand in mine.

I love you. 💜
